Container functionality

Headers that have fields provide limited container-style access to their fields.

The sam::header::iterator and sam::header::const_iterator classes are bidirectional iterators providing all the usual iterator functionality: copying, assignment, pre- and post-increment and -decrement, equality and inequality tests, and dereferencing, which produces a header::tagfield through which the pointed-to field's properties can be accessed (but not modified; see also the iterator variant of set_field() below).
