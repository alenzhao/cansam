Enumeration Type Documentation

enum sam::alignment_flag

The flags field of an alignment contains a number of flag bits pertaining to the read that has been aligned and a few pertaining to its mate.

Enumerator:
PAIRED 

The read is paired with a mate.

PROPER_PAIRED 

The read and its mate are mapped as a proper pair.

UNMAPPED 

The read is unmapped.

MATE_UNMAPPED 

The mate is unmapped.

REVERSE_STRAND 

The read's strand (0: forward; 1: reverse).

MATE_REVERSE_STRAND 

The mate's strand (0: forward; 1: reverse).

FIRST_IN_PAIR 

The read is the first of a pair.

SECOND_IN_PAIR 

The read is the second of a pair.

NONPRIMARY 

This alignment is not the primary one for the read.

QUALITY_FAILED 

Failed platform or vendor quality checks.

DUPLICATE 

PCR duplicate or optical duplicate.
