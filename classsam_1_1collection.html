Container functionality

Collections provide limited container-style access to their headers and reference sequences.

The various iterator classes are random access iterators providing all the usual iterator functionality. Dereferencing an iterator or const_iterator produces a sam::header, while dereferencing a ref_iterator or const_ref_iterator produces a sam::refsequence.
